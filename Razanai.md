
## Party's Knowledge
The Razanai the extinct race of builders. Legend says that these builders made old temples, caverns, and even mountains. These builders built structures that became one with nature, but have now become lost, and no one can find them. 
Not much else is known about these builders. Some believe that these builders could speak things into existence. There are certain researchers that would pay top coin to anyone who will find a structure for them to research.

Some believe these builders were real, but the stories are greatly exaggerated, while some don't care, or believe they never existed at all.
