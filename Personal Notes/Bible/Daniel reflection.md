Daniel has a lot of faith in God, even when he's in danger or doesn't understand. In the last chapters of Daniel, he is told prophecies that he can't understand the meaning of, but must continue to trust Him, and continue on his way until the very end. I should be trusting God with everything I have. I often think I'm putting things in His hands, but I'm still holding onto them. I'm not letting God handle my burden, I'm trying to make it my problem, and have God on the side, when that's not what He wants from me. He's told me that I need to give it *all* to Him, but I haven't paid attention to His instructions, and I'm suffering because of it.
