1. Deluxe Purple
	- turns into a vial that is functionally Venom

2. Deluxe Yellow
	- turns into the [[The Eye of the Familiar]]

3. Deluxe Green
	- allows character to do one of the following at a time:
		- remember 1 shape to morph into, and shapeshift into it at any point (1/day)
		- use polymorph spell (1/day)

4. Deluxe Orange
	- functionally becomes [[Mirakuru]] 

5. Deluxe Red
	- turns into a tattoo/visible mark that can automatically succeed on an intimidation roll 1/long rest

6. Deluxe Blue
	- turns into a diadem that is functionally a normal potion, but can be used as an action, bonus action, or reaction to being attacked. can only use it on yourself
