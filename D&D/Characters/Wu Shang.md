BACKSTORY:  
When Wu Shang turned 40 on his birthday, his village (Enyama) was attacked by a band of rogue Dragonborn bounty hunters at a count of 7. His village at a count of 55 (+3 pregnant including his wife) were almost swept away by the bounty hunters, but Wu Shang hid 7 villagers that survived. He killed 4 hunters and one of the 3 left killed his wife and unborn child by decapitating her while Wu Shang was killing the 4 bounty hunters. No doctors in his village could figure out how to save the child, so the child died. Wu Shang swore an oath of revenge and sought 2 elders of great knowledge. One of combat and one of magic. Wu Shang is still seeking the hunter that killed his wife and unborn child, but had settled in a small city (Yuno) as an elder. He's aided in defending the city from attacks, but few common folk have died under his watch. The villagers seek his wisdom every now and then, but only one person sought out his skill. A young woman by the name of KUNA TAI asked him to teach her how to fight. After some time, he agreed and became known as Sensei Wu Shang. Some villagers have began to wonder how he looks younger than his age, but he has refused to tell anyone.  
  
APPEARANCE:  
Appearing in his early 40s, he has straight, long, black and neatly kept hair, a long, slightly curly black beard of approximately one foot of length bound by two golden rings at the end and the middle.  
Wears a tattered scarf around his head and neck as a hood, and wears worn and stained rags that show a glimpse of hidden shiny chainmail underneath.  
Brown eyes.  
  
MISC:  
Can live forever because of [[Seed of Eden]] but is still vulnerable to death by a mortal wound or incurable disease. Still requires sustenance, but can last a lot longer without it.
  
ANALYSIS/GIMMICK:  
Very experienced and balanced in magic and martial arts/combat. Can theoretically live forever. Seeks revenge.
