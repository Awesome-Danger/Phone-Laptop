BACKSTORY:  
When Kuna Tai was born, she was taken in as a foundling by a Dragonborn bounty hunter. His last raid gone wrong, he had fled, defeated by [[Wu Shang]]. Kuna Tai was Taken from her mother as soon as she was born, and her mother was killed by the Dragonborn that had raised her. Kuna Tai ran away from the Dragonborn's camp when she learned of this. She had never gone too far from it before, so she lost her way, and arrived at a small city named YUNO and discovered an elder there and learned how to control her fear and was taught much in combat, but little on magic. She now travels from city to city and village to village as a mercenary for hire. She charges according to the job and at an affordable price too... if she doesn't hate you.  
  
APPEARANCE:  
Straight long brown hair, neatly kept in a bun with what appears to be two large silver needles.  
Wears leather armor with steel plates on a couple pieces of armor and both bracers.  
Caries a sharp double tapered silver pole that never seems to loose its point no matter what she stabs.  
Green eyes.  
  
ANALYSIS/GIMMICK:  
Very experienced in combat, but not magic. Seeks revenge.
