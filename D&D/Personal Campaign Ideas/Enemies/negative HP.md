An event with a character where they figure out that when they hit 0 HP, or lower while in hell, or something, they are still alive, and can fight and act like normal.The player(s) who this happens to need to stay at negative or zero points to stay alive.

Later, a boss-like being consumes the parties traumatic experiences. If a player is at full HP, it takes damage equivalent to their HP value. If they are at partial health, the being gains HP equivalent to (max HP - current HP). 

This means that if a player who doesn't die when in the negatives, it will gain a lot of HP, so it would be a better idea for them to stay close or at zero HP, but they don't know that ;)
