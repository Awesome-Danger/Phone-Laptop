• This eye is made of unbreakable obsidian with a jade iris and can heal the mind of anybody that looks into it no matter how broken or beaten little or large. This works on all mammals, but not insects. Animals of all kinds are pacified by the presence of the eye, and will often accompany the wielder of the wielder so wishes.
