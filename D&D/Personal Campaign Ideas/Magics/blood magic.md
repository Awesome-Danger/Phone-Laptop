Blood magic is a type of [[dark magic]] that uses blood as a sacrificial currency for certain powerful magics. Blood from different sources is worth something different. An ounce of cow blood is worth much less than an ounce of human blood, even though humans outnumber cows greatly. In fact, 1 ounce of average human blood is roughly equivalent of up to 5 to 7 GALLONS of cow blood. This is because the potential of the sacrifice is so much greater for humans than cows. Humans change the world and have much potential for influence, and cows do not. The blood of a young man has more value than the blood of an old one, though not by as considerable of an amount. Old blood has its uses in certain rituals, but its value in performing spells is usually less. In certain circumstances, such as when someone has lived a long, healthy life for 100 years and can live another 100, that blood would be incredibly valuable. Rarity can affect value to a degree; the value raises exponentially for anything, but if it's not worth much to begin with, it won't matter how rare it is.
