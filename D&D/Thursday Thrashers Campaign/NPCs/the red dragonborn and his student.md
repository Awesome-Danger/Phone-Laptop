[[Theodosia]] met these people in [[session 4]] while looking for help. She asked if they knew what the [[Creed|symbol]] meant on Bjorn's sickle. The teacher answered, saying "yes, that symbol is well known among our kind, though I have retired. [Youngling], why don't you tell her?" The student fails to answer, saying he doesn't remember. Theo asks him where she can find the [[Creed]], but he simply answers "You don't find them, they find you. Look for something... strange..." He walks out of the tavern without another word.
