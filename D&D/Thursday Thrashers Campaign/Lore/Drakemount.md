Drakemount is a country with several cities that each hold one of the strands. The capitol city is [[Epitath]], and the others are [[Memoria]], [[Affirmadia]], [[Shalamba]], and *two other unnamed cities*. [[King Aldric]] was the king of Drakemount for many years until he was decapitated by [[Hellion]], who sought out the strands, and now pretends to be king Aldric. Drakemount has a decently skilled and sized navy, and military.
