
- In the prison, Bjorn finds himself alone in a cell that's fireproof, and with a steel ball-and-chain shackled around his ankle. Bjorn finds a trained dog holding the keys; learns its shape to transform into it, making his fitted shackle fall off; calls the dog over to make a deal with it. The dog drops the keys, and [Bjorn sets everyone free,] and they get their gear back from nearby cubbies. The [king has taken all the gold,] however. After the dog sees Bjorn shift back into his dragonborn shape, takes off, and isn't seen again.
- The *halls are strangely empty,* with no guards and not much light. The group goes down the hall toward a split and to the left, Bjorn and Ulfgar slightly see and hear this strange limping figure walking down the dark corridor. They meet the figure in the hallway, and as they come closer, they discover it's undead, and has many acid and fire burns. Bjorn realizes it's one of the [Browning Brothers' goons!] Bjorn kills him again, and the group continues exploring.
- Bjorn drags his right hand along the wall to avoid getting lost, and as the group goes through these halls, they meet a few more of these undead goons, who are easy enough to kill. They find a room with four chests. Bjorn discovers an [obsidian dagger], but can't decipher anything special about it.
- After more traversing, the group finds themselves beneath a room of fully armed guards going about their business, and isn't spotted. They decide to go back the other way, and find a [hidden ledger room.] Bjorn and Ulfgar look through the letters, and Bjorn pockets a [unsigned letter] and a [crime record.] after reading them, but hasn't decided to tell the group what they said yet. Through more hidden walls, the group finds a closet with tally marks, and a separate corridor.
- Through the corridor, the group stumbles upon a room [dimly lit with green flames,] with a [summoning circle] on the floor. They see the [Browning Brothers], dead, but stitched together in the middle of the circle. The circle activates, and the Browning Brothers Start to stagger to their feet.
