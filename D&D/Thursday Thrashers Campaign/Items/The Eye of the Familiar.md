- This eye is a smooth, durable quartz ball with a dim, warm, orange glowing sphere in the center of the inside of it. The sphere contains within it a familiar that takes a single shape of the user's choosing one time only, and then cannot change. This familiar also has an identical fake eye in the corresponding eye. This familiar functions like most familiars, except your soul partially inhabits this companion, meaning that you share HP, thoughts, and can see out of the other's fake eye. This familiar only knows the languages the user does, and cannot understand any other including its natural tongue. The familiar can be summoned at will (incantation), and has a range of 300ft (100 yards).
