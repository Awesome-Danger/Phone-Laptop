If the wielder gives into the voices, great strength will be granted to them at the cost of their sanity. The user rolls for luck when initiating it’s ability, and the user’s granted +5 STR, +3 INT, -5 CHA, -3 PER. To attempt to deactivate the ability, make a constitution saving throw. The inverse of the initial luck roll is the minimum of the constitution saving throw (5 luck = 15 Con save, 1 luck = 20 Con save, 20 luck = 1 Con save, etc.)
