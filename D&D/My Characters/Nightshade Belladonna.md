CHARACTER: Nightshade/Belladonna
FULL NAME: Sarah Beau-Kin
SEX: Female
AGE: 30
HEIGHT: 5' 10"
RACE: HUMAN 
CLASS: Bard

BACKSTORY:

Sarah was 6 years old when she got her first lute. Her father had died when he was murdered in cold blood by a member of a secret cartel because he betrayed the cartel by spreading word of it through his songs. Sarah was given her father's lute right after he died. In his honor, she practiced the lute with her father's old teacher. Sarah was a fast learner, and still is to this day, so she became even better than her father ever was and provided for her mother when she grew up with the earnings she made playing her own songs. She also did some side hustles with the magic she learned from her mother's education of spells. She was the only other magician in her town. When she was 18, her mother died of illness, and sharing her mother's passion of travel she sailed the seas to spread her music as far as she could. She sailed with a few different groups of sailors in her time at sea. Her time with the sailors were filled with many storms and hard labor, but she learned how to sail a ship quickly on her first trip across the sea. Her earnings as a bard appeared to not be enough for sailing the sea, so she labored just as hard as any of the other sailors to earn her passage. She often played her lute for the sailors. Her name was spread to civilians and sailors alike across the seas, and became known as Nightshade or Belladonna because she often attracts men to her, and kills them silently with poison, which she had taught herself.

APPEARANCE:
Beautiful appearance. Often wears sailor's attire when traveling. Deceptively sleek muscle build.

ANALYSIS/GIMMICK:
Just as handy with a lute as a dagger. Fast learner, familiar with sailors and magic. Seeks fame and fortune.
