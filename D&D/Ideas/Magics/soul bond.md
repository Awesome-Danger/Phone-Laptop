A soul bond is an occurrence between a person's soul and any noun (person, place, thing, or idea). The more specific/narrow the noun is, the easier it is to bind your soul to that noun. If you attempt to bind yourself to a general wider noun, *you will die- success or failure.* If you succeed you will become that general noun, and your mind will never be the same, but you may control anything that has been assigned to that general noun in any way. (e.g. if you bound your soul to the general noun chair you would be anything that was honestly called/considered a chair). If you bind yourself to a specific person, you can communicate with them through your mind no matter the distance or what's in-between. Difference in language means nothing and you're unable to lie to each other. Your entire being is revealed to them all at once, so they should know you exceedingly well before being bound. Being bound to someone and being unable to hide any part of your being from your soul bound partner can drive some mad, so take caution. If the specific thing you bound your soul to gets destroyed/dies you may die as well. Most don't survive their soul being ripped away from the mortal realm. Those who do typically go insane or at least lose some marbles (or if one dies the living one might draw the other part into their body and have a split personality). If someone binds their soul with someone who was so bound to another object already, they gain about equal control of the things the other person was bound to. There are certain rules for [[soulbound twins]].
