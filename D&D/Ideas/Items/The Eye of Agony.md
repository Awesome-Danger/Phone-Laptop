- This eye is made of unbreakable obsidian with a ruby iris and will torture the mind of anyone who looks into it for the equivalent of a thousand years no matter how long or short you look at it. Once you know what you see, it's over. Everybody this has been used on has been left babbling like a newborn until the rest of their days. It's been theorized that the effect on immortals would be lessened depending on how long they have already lived. This only seems to work on humans, but animals seem to sense its presence, and flee.
