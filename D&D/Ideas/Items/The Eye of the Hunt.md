- A sphere made entirely out of a strange black crystal, this eye, only when inserted into a sentient being's empty eye socket, can lock onto one soul, being able to see its essence through any amount of material, or any distance. This strange crystal treasure does not belong to the mortal realm however, so it's being searched for by spirits in the realm it came from while you use its ability, or if it's seen by those spirits. This eye is invisible to other spirits.
