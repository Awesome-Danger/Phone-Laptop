#Call-of-Cthulhu Oneshot?

Somebody onboard has been *infected with*, or *is* a Eldritch being that is causing strange happenings onboard the Titanic.

Arrangement of Among Us Theme in the style of Ludwig the Holy Blade

Two ways to do this: 
- make an NPC the Eldritch being
- a real player is the impostor.

Ends in one of these ways:

- The Eldritch being causes the Titanic to sink by crashing it into something, or attacking it in some way. [bad]
- Titanic is taken to another plane, never to be seen again (short campaign material?) (try to get out of it, but when you do, it's not the right one.) [neutral]
- Titanic is saved from the eldritch being, but crashes into an iceberg and sinks. [good]
