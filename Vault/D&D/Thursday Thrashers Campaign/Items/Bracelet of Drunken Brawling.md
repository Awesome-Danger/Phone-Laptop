If the user is drunk, and breaks a chair in a fight, the chair will rebuild itself forcefully back together to a central point piece by piece. The pieces are drawn to the piece with the most physical resistance toward the central point (e.g. If a leg in in the user’s hand, and the rest are scattered on the floor, the pieces will rebuild from the piece in the user’s hand). An eligible chair is made of wood, has 4 legs, a seat, a back, and seats one average sized person or child of the user’s race.
