#item 

- A sapphire ring with the mind of Brom the Servant of Ranii. To awaken the ring's mind, transfer and store in it 100 hp collectively.
- Once Brom is revived in his ring from, he can cast certain spells, and has his own initiative.
- He can communicate telepathically in any language with any creature within 150 ft without restriction of materials that lie between the ring and the subject.

> [!NOTE]
> This ring can store energy that comes from the user within its sapphire. Legend says this ring has been secretly used by a few sorcerers as a fuel for magic without spending their own energy in the midst of battle. Could come in handy… The player can fill the perfect sapphire in this ring with any amount of energy from their health to store the energy for certain spells to be cast instantly (no player turn is taken) at any point later on. The ring will fit perfectly around anyone’s finger, and in its current state, the ring cannot be taken off. Requires attunement.

- The Ring of Brom is an entity. The Sapphire contains the soul of Ranii, who can cast spells in his current form, but needs to be given health points to be used. If 100 points of health are transferred into the sapphire at once, it will awaken the unconscious mind inside the ring.
