A stone that when touched directly, changes you into a different version, or type of your race or a race that is somewhat related to it (perhaps even races that aren’t normally playable). E.g., a human could become a Tiefling; A Dragonborn to a Dragon Kin, or other reptilian race; an Orc to a Giant; A True Dragon to a Dragonborn. Animals would become animals that are similar; however, familiars, or summons are not affected by this stone, and can touch it directly without any change. This item is consumed on use.
