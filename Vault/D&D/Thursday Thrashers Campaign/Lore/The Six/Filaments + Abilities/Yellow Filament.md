
[[III (Yellow)]]

- Monkey's Paw
	- Like a normal monkey's paw, there are 5 wishes that the DM can choose how it will horribly go wrong.
	- You get something special if you use all of the wishes on the paw. You'll find it in the clutches of the closed hand:
		- [[Deluxe Yellow]]

###### Benefits

- resistance to Radiant damage
- add 10ft to movement speed
- advantage on Dexterity (acrobatics) checks


###### Spells and Actions

CHOICE

- Cleric
Radiant Cord
	As a cantrip, create a golden colored Cord of up to 50 ft in length. The rope is 2x stronger and lighter than normal rope; can be cut into as many pieces as the caster wants at will; cast again to make the cord disappear.
	Can choose for rope to act as a whip, doing 2d4 Radiant damage.
	Rope can also be used to grapple enemies; target must pass a Strength check of 14 to not be grappled. The target can make a Strength check of 14 at the beginning of their turns; if succeeded, they are no longer grappled.
- 10 minutes concentration.
	- presented as: [a golden rope]
	Cure Wounds: always have cure wounds prepared (functionally gives it as another spell, making it pointless for a cleric to prepare it from their own list)
	- presented as: [a scroll with a health Potion on it]

- Warrior
	Time Stutter: as a magic action, create a short pause in time that causes target(s) to be unable to use a reaction for the next turn.
	- Can be cast once per short rest.
		- presented as: [a strange looking clock]
	Radiance
	Reaction: can choose for any melee attack within 30 ft to do radiant damage instead.
	- presented as: [a mini sun, with a soft yellow glow]
