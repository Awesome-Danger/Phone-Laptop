
[[III (Yellow)]]

- Monkey's Paw
	- Like a normal monkey's paw, there are 5 wishes that the DM can choose how it will horribly go wrong.
	- You get something special if you use all of the wishes on the paw. You'll find it in the clutches of the closed hand:
		- [[Deluxe Yellow]]

##### Benefits

- resistance to Radiant damage
- add 10 ft to movement speed
- advantage on Dexterity (acrobatics) checks


##### Spells

Radiant Cord
	As a cantrip, create a golden colored Cord of up to 50 ft in length. The rope is 2x stronger and lighter than normal rope; can be cut into as many pieces as the caster wants at will; cast again to make the cord disappear.
	Can choose for cord to act as a whip, doing 2d4 Radiant damage.
	Cord can also be used to grapple enemies; target must pass a Strength check of 14 to not be grappled. The target can make a Strength check of 14 at the beginning of their turns; if succeeded, they are no longer grappled.
	10 minutes concentration.
	presented as: [a golden cord]

Time Stutter 
	as a magic action, create a short pause in time that causes target(s) to be unable to use a reaction for the next turn.
	Can be cast once per short rest.
	presented as: [a strange looking clock]
