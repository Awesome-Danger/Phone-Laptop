
Wondrous Item, uncommon (requires attunement by a 1 level in Warlock)

BENEFITS:
• Your movement speed increases by 10 ft.
• You gain resistance to force damage.
• You have advantage on checks made to resist being grappled, shoved, or moved unwillingly.

----------------------------------------------------------

Gravitic Lash
Uses: 3 charges per Long Rest 
Activation: Action
Range: 60 ft

- [x] 1
- [ ] 2
- [ ] 3

You fire a crackling filament of purple tether-energy at a creature you can see.
The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw (DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier).

On a failed save:
You pull the creature up to 20 ft straight toward you.
If it ends within 5 ft of you, it becomes Grappled (escape DC equals item save DC).
While grappled this way, the creature has Its speed reduced to 0 and takes 2d6 force damage at the start of each of its turns.

It may repeat the save at the end of each of its turns to escape.

On a success: The creature is not pulled, but takes half the force damage.

------------------------------------------------------------
ANCHOR WEB
Uses: 1 charge (regains at long rest)
Activation: Bonus Action
Range: 30 ft

- [x] 1

Place an anchor point of violet filament. At the start of your next turn,
choose up to 3 creatures within 15 ft of the anchor.

Each must make a Dexterity saving throw.

On a failure:
• The creature is Restrained.
• Its speed is 0.
• It cannot teleport or turn invisible.
• Forced movement moves the creature half as far.

On a success:
• The creature’s speed is halved until the end of your next turn.

----------------------------------------------------------


Notes: Resistance: Force, Bonus: Speed, 1 level in Warlock
