In a world that has been at peace for hundreds of years, a tone in the atmosphere begins to change. The King is no longer seen in the streets helping his people. Money and food have become more scares, and all you can wander is, what happened to the “men of the people?”

One dark night, you are walking through the streets close to midnight when you notice a prismatic light shoot up from the Castle. As you more to the castle to see What it is, you notice the gates are open. Upon inspection you notice the castle is empty and the only noise you hear is coming from the upstairs. As you move up, you reach the top where you see your once loved king Roped to a chair as a man walks around him.

“Where are they old man?” asks the man circling the chair.
“ I already told you, every city has their own, and you have already ruined your chances at g collecting them by posing as me. All the leaders know that I would never ask for them.”  Responds the king.
“I need not to convince anyone if I know where they are,” replies the stranger.
“Hellion, listen to me. What you are doing is rash and will only end in death.”
“you are no use to me old man,” responds Hellion in a calm, sophisticated tone.
 Horror flashes in your eyes as you see the standing man creates a spectral blade and slice off the king's head. 
“Find the strands, by any means necessary. The master requires us to stay on schedule,” Hellion says as his troops begin to head in your direction. You run as fast as possible out of the castle. Uncertain of what you just saw, you pack your bag with only one thing on your mind. 
You may not know what the “strands” are, but you need to find them first.
