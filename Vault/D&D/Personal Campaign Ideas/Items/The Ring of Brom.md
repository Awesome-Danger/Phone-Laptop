- A sapphire ring with the mind of Brom the Servant of Ranii. To awaken the ring's mind, transfer and store in it 100 hp collectively.
- Once Brom is revived in his ring from, he can cast certain spells, and has his own initiative.
- He can communicate telepathically in any language with any creature within 150 ft without restriction of materials that lie between the ring and the subject.
