- Vampires are magicians that choose/chose a darker path of magic and have to make sacrifices of blood to perform magic. Their bodies became dead but didn't rot. That was thanks to the magic that binds them and that they can't make new blood. Vampires magically grow fangs that aren't just for puncturing, but lead directly to the circulatory system, which is now used not to maintain their bodies, but just to store the blood they need for [[dark magic]] Spells/incantations.
