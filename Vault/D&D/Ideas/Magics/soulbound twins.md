Twins are naturally [[soul bond|Soulbound]]. Though the link is weaker, they still have certain connections to each other. Additionally, if the other dies, one will still live on, even if they soul bound themselves to each other through magic as well. Magic sees identical twins as almost the same person. If one of the twins were to bind themselves to something, the other twin would also somewhat have control over what was soulbound to their twin. If their twin died after being bound to something, the living twin would inherit full control of a physical soulbound object. If the twin died while binding their soul to a general idea, the twin would live as long as they weren't magically bound together.
